## How it works

ZeroGTM runs on your infra (or our hosted tier). You connect APIs and webhooks; we don’t store your lead data in a third-party cloud. **BYOK** (bring your own API keys) is supported for Google Maps, email finder, and enrichment—so you control cost and compliance.
